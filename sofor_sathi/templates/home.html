Our Mission: Your Trustworthy Companion

"Sofor Sathi" means "Travel Companion." This isn't just a name‚Äîit's our promise. We created this platform to solve a real problem: finding reliable, all-in-one travel information for Bangladesh is too difficult.

We were tired of jumping between different blogs, social media groups, and confusing websites just to plan a single trip. We knew there had to be a better, simpler, and more "humanize" way.

Our mission is to be the single source of truth for travelers. We combine detailed location guides, practical route information, and an honest review system with powerful planning tools. We are building a platform where you can go from a vague idea to a fully planned trip with confidence, all in one place.

Built By Travelers, For Travelers

The best travel guides are written by the travelers themselves. Sofor Sathi is a community-driven platform that gets better with every new user.

Share Your Experience
